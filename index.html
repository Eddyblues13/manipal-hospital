Leading Healthcare Excellence Since 1990

Manipal Teaching Hospital stands as a beacon of medical excellence, combining compassionate patient care with innovative medical education and groundbreaking research.

Our state-of-the-art facility houses over 500 beds, advanced diagnostic equipment, and specialized treatment centers. We serve as both a premier healthcare provider and a distinguished medical education institution, training the next generation of healthcare professionals.

  • Basic Patient Care Techniques
  • Vital Signs Monitoring
  • Healthcare Safety Protocols
  • Medical Equipment Handling
  • Patient Communication Skills
  • Emergency Response Training

Auxiliary Nursing & Midwifery

2 Years

Specialized program for nursing assistants focusing exclusively on maternal and child healthcare, assisting midwives in delivery rooms and postnatal care units.

  • Maternal Healthcare Assistance
  • Delivery Room Support
  • Newborn Care Procedures
  • Postnatal Care Assistance
  • Midwifery Support Skills
  • Child Health Monitoring

Wound Care Nursing

2 Years

Comprehensive two-year program in advanced wound management, pressure ulcer prevention, and specialized foot care techniques for complex patient cases.

  • Advanced Wound Assessment
  • Complex Dressing Techniques
  • Diabetic Foot Care Management
  • Pressure Ulcer Prevention
  • Surgical Wound Care
  • Infection Control Protocols
