Leading Healthcare Excellence Since 1990

Manipal Teaching Hospital stands as a beacon of medical excellence, combining compassionate patient care with innovative medical education and groundbreaking research.

Our state-of-the-art facility houses over 500 beds, advanced diagnostic equipment, and specialized treatment centers. We serve as both a premier healthcare provider and a distinguished medical education institution, training the next generation of healthcare professionals.
