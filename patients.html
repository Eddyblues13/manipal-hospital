Admission Process

Our streamlined admission process ensures you receive timely care with minimal paperwork.

1
Registration

Complete patient registration at our reception desk with valid ID and insurance details.

2
Consultation

Meet with our specialist for diagnosis and treatment planning.

3
Admission

Complete admission formalities and room allocation based on your preference and medical needs.

Visitor Guidelines

  • Follow infection control protocols
