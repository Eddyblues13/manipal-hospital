Eligibility Criteria

Academic Requirements
  • MBBS degree from a recognized medical college
  • Completion of compulsory rotating internship
  • Permanent registration with MCI/State Medical Council
  • Valid NEET-PG score card
Entrance Examination

All candidates must qualify the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test for Postgraduate courses (NEET-PG) conducted by the National Board of Examinations (NBE).

  • Valid NEET-PG score is mandatory
  • NEET-PG typically conducted in January
  • Computer-based test at designated centers
  • Application through online counseling

Admission Process

1

NEET-PG Registration

Register for NEET-PG examination through the official NBE website. Complete the application process and download the admit card.

2

NEET-PG Examination

Appear for the computer-based NEET-PG examination at the designated test center on the scheduled date.

3

Result Declaration

Check your NEET-PG results, score, and All India Rank on the official website. The results are typically declared within one month.

4

Counseling Registration

Register for the centralized counseling process through the designated counseling authority website.

5

Choice Filling & Seat Allotment

Fill your preferred choices of colleges and specialties. Seat allotment is done based on NEET-PG rank, choices, and availability.

6

Admission Formalities

Complete document verification, pay the fees, and report to the allotted college to begin your postgraduate program.
