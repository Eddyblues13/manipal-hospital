Our Specialized Departments

State-of-the-art medical care across all specialties with comprehensive diagnostic, therapeutic, and surgical capabilities. Our departments are equipped with the latest technology and staffed by experienced medical professionals dedicated to providing exceptional patient care.

Cardiology & Cardiac Surgery

Our Cardiology Department provides comprehensive cardiac care with state-of-the-art diagnostic and therapeutic facilities. We specialize in both non-invasive and invasive cardiology procedures, offering complete heart care from prevention to complex surgical interventions. Our team of cardiologists and cardiac surgeons work collaboratively to provide personalized treatment plans for each patient.

Comprehensive Services Offered:
  • Cardiac Catheterization: Advanced diagnostic and interventional procedures
  • Angioplasty & Stenting: Minimally invasive coronary interventions
  • Cardiac Bypass Surgery: Coronary artery bypass grafting (CABG)
  • Pacemaker Implantation: Single and dual chamber devices
  • Echocardiography: 2D, 3D, and stress echocardiography
  • Cardiac Rehabilitation: Comprehensive post-treatment recovery programs

Neurology & Neurosurgery

Our Neurology Department offers comprehensive care for disorders of the brain, spine, and nervous system with advanced diagnostic and therapeutic capabilities. We provide multidisciplinary approach to neurological conditions, combining medical management with advanced surgical interventions when necessary. Our team includes neurologists, neurosurgeons, and rehabilitation specialists working together for optimal patient outcomes.

Advanced Neurological Services:
  • Stroke Management: Comprehensive stroke care with thrombolysis
  • Brain & Spine Surgery: Minimally invasive and complex procedures
  • Epilepsy Treatment: Advanced monitoring and management
  • Neurological Rehabilitation: Comprehensive recovery programs
  • EEG & EMG Studies: Advanced neurophysiological testing
  • Movement Disorder Clinic: Specialized Parkinson's care

Pediatrics & Neonatology

Comprehensive healthcare for children from birth through adolescence, with specialized neonatal intensive care for premature and critically ill newborns. Our pediatric department provides family-centered care in a child-friendly environment, addressing both common childhood illnesses and complex medical conditions. We emphasize preventive care, growth monitoring, and developmental support.

Pediatric & Neonatal Services:
  • Level III NICU: Advanced neonatal intensive care unit
  • Pediatric Surgery: Specialized surgical care for children
  • Vaccination Services: Complete immunization schedule
  • Growth & Development Monitoring: Regular developmental assessments
  • Pediatric Emergency Care: 24/7 emergency services for children
  • Adolescent Medicine: Specialized care for teenagers
  • Pediatric Cardiology: Heart care for children
  • Child Psychology Services: Mental health support
