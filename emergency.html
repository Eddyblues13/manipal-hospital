24/7 Emergency Department

Our Emergency Department is staffed round the clock by experienced emergency physicians, nurses, and support staff to provide immediate medical care for all types of emergencies.

We are equipped to handle medical, surgical, pediatric, obstetric, and trauma emergencies with state-of-the-art facilities and rapid response protocols.
