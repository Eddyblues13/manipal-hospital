MBBS Program Overview

5.5 Years of Comprehensive Medical Education

The MBBS program at Manipal Teaching Hospital is designed to produce competent, compassionate medical professionals equipped with the knowledge and skills to excel in the healthcare industry. Our curriculum combines rigorous academic training with extensive clinical exposure.

  • MCI Recognized Program
  • International Accreditation
  • Early Clinical Exposure
  • Research Opportunities
  • Modern Simulation Labs
  • International Electives
  • Community Health Programs
  • 1000+ Bed Teaching Hospital

Eligibility Criteria

Academic Requirements
  • Completed 10+2 or equivalent examination with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks in PCB for General category (40% for reserved categories)
  • Minimum 17 years of age as of December 31st of the admission year
  • Indian Nationals, NRIs, and Foreign Nationals are eligible to apply
Entrance Examination

All candidates must qualify the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET-UG) conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA).

  • Valid NEET-UG score is mandatory for admission
  • NEET-UG is typically conducted in May each year
  • Application through online counseling process

Admission Process

1

NEET-UG Examination

Appear for the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET-UG) conducted by NTA. Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria and prepare thoroughly for the examination.

2

Result Declaration

Check your NEET-UG results and All India Rank. The results are typically declared within one month of the examination date.

3

Counseling Registration

Register for the centralized counseling process through the official counseling authority website. Choice filling and locking is a crucial step in this process.

4

Seat Allotment

Based on your NEET rank, choices filled, and seat availability, you will be allotted a seat in the counseling rounds.

5

Document Verification

Report to the designated center with all original documents for verification. This is a mandatory process for final admission.

6

Admission Confirmation

Complete the admission formalities, pay the fees, and join the institution on the specified date to begin your medical journey.
