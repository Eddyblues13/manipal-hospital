Research & Innovation

Advancing medical science through cutting-edge research and innovation

Research Centers of Excellence

Our institution is committed to advancing medical knowledge through innovative research across various specialties. We foster a culture of inquiry and discovery that translates into improved patient care and medical education.

Cardiovascular Research Center

Our cardiovascular research unit conducts pioneering clinical trials and research in interventional cardiology, cardiac surgery, and preventive cardiology. Current projects include studies on novel anticoagulants, minimally invasive cardiac procedures, and population-based heart disease prevention strategies.

Clinical Trials Interventional Research Preventive Cardiology
Neuroscience Research Unit

Advanced studies in stroke management, neurodegenerative disorders, and neurosurgical techniques. Our neuroscience team collaborates with international institutions on research involving neuroimaging, neuropharmacology, and innovative surgical approaches for complex neurological conditions.

Stroke Research Neurodegeneration Surgical Innovation
Oncology Research Institute

Comprehensive oncology research focusing on targeted therapies, early detection methods, and personalized medicine approaches. Our cancer research program includes molecular oncology, clinical cancer genetics, and development of novel therapeutic protocols for various malignancies.

Targeted Therapy Early Detection Personalized Medicine
Infectious Diseases Research

Cutting-edge research in epidemiology, antimicrobial resistance, and vaccine development. Our infectious diseases team works on emerging pathogens, hospital-acquired infections, and public health interventions for disease control and prevention.

Antimicrobial Resistance Vaccine Research Epidemiology
