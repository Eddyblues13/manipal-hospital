Our Story

Founded in 1990, Manipal Teaching Hospital has been at the forefront of healthcare excellence and medical education in India for over three decades.

What began as a vision to provide world-class healthcare to our community has evolved into one of India's most respected teaching hospitals. We combine cutting-edge medical technology with compassionate care, while simultaneously nurturing the next generation of healthcare professionals.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, expansion of services, and an unwavering commitment to patient care. Today, we stand as a beacon of hope for thousands of patients and a center of learning for aspiring medical professionals.
