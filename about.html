About Us

A Trusted Center for Healthcare and Medical Education

Since our establishment, we have been dedicated to providing quality healthcare while nurturing the next generation of medical professionals. Our institution combines compassionate patient care, advanced medical technologies, and a strong commitment to education and research.

With highly specialized departments, modern facilities, and expert faculty, we continue to serve the community as a leading medical institution focused on healing, learning, and innovation.

Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Independent Excellence: Our Journey

Our story began in 1990 when we first established our medical institution through an affiliation with a local hospital. During those formative years, we worked collaboratively to build our foundation in medical education and patient care, learning valuable lessons about healthcare delivery and institutional management.

For over a decade, this partnership allowed us to develop our teaching methodologies and clinical practices while serving the community under the guidance of our affiliate institution. However, our vision always extended beyond collaboration â€” we aspired to create an independent center of medical excellence that could fully embody our values and innovative approach to healthcare.

In 2005, we reached a pivotal milestone when our institution emerged as an independent teaching hospital. This transition marked the beginning of a new era, allowing us to implement our unique educational philosophy, expand our specialized services, and establish our own standards of clinical excellence without the constraints of our previous affiliation.

Since gaining our independence, we have flourished into a premier healthcare and education destination, known for our innovative treatments, research contributions, and commitment to training the next generation of medical leaders. Our journey from affiliate to autonomous institution reflects our unwavering dedication to advancing healthcare on our own terms.
